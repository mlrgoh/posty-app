# Why I Like Coding

By Richard G

- Problem solving: breaking down complex tasks into manageable steps is satisfying.
- Continuous learning: there are always new tools and techniques to learn.
- Creativity: writing code lets me design elegant solutions and craft user experiences.
- Feedback loop: running and iterating quickly shows tangible progress.
